Shirahama Beach – Wakayama’s White Sand Paradise

Shirahama Beach in Wakayama is one of Japan’s most iconic resort destinations. Known for its soft white sand, clear blue water, and easy access from Osaka, it’s perfect for first-time beach travelers.

If you want a beach close to Tokyo with a relaxed vibe, Zushi Beach is a top pick. It offers calm waves, a younger crowd, and a great view of Mount Fuji on clear days.

Jodogahama Beach – Iwate’s Scenic Rocky Jewel

Located on the Sanriku Coast in northern Japan, Jodogahama Beach is famous for its dramatic rock formations and glass-clear water. It’s not a usual sandy beach—more of a scenic coastal wonder.

Emerald Beach – Okinawa’s Spacious, Family-Friendly Coast

Located inside the Ocean Expo Park near the famous Churaumi Aquarium, Emerald Beach offers bright water, soft sand, and a clean, organized environment.

Yuigahama Beach – Kamakura’s Popular Surfing Spot

Yuigahama is Kamakura’s most active beach—perfect for travelers who enjoy a mix of culture and surfing. After the beach, you can visit shrines, cafés, and Kamakura’s historical streets.
